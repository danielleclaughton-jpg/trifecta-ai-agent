## Target Audience Profile

### Who They Are
- **Job titles**: Drillers, engineers, operators, camp supervisors, field technicians, safety officers, project managers
- **Companies**: Suncor, Syncrude, CNRL, Cenovus, Enbridge, TC Energy, MEG Energy, Imperial Oil
- **Location hubs**: Fort McMurray, Edmonton, Calgary, Red Deer, Grande Prairie, Lloydminster
- **Schedule**: Rotational — typically 2 weeks on-site, 1-2 weeks off
- **Income**: $100K–$300K+
- **Age**: 28–55
- **Concerns**: Job security, career confidentiality, stigma, no time for in-person treatment

### Why They Need Trifecta
1. High-stress, isolated work environments drive substance use
2. Camp culture normalizes heavy drinking/stimulant use
3. Rotational schedule makes traditional outpatient impossible
4. Drug/alcohol violations = immediate termination (massive career risk)
5. No privacy in small camp communities — they won't seek local help
6. Mental health challenges (depression, anxiety, PTSD) often co-occur

### What They Want to Hear
- **No career interruption** — treatment fits around their rotation
- **Complete confidentiality** — employer will never know unless they choose to disclose
- **Evidence-based, not AA-style** — they respond to science and practicality
- **Quick, structured** — 28-day program, clear schedule, measurable outcomes
- **Accessed from camp or home** — virtual-first, no commuting

## Lead Tracking — Oil & Gas Database

Oil & Gas leads are stored in `oil_gas_leads.db` with the following fields:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| `id` | Unique lead ID |
| `name` | Lead name |
| `email` | Lead email |
| `phone` | Lead phone |
| `source` | Source (default: `oil_gas_website`) |
| `platform` | Platform where lead came from |
| `enquiry_type` | Type of inquiry (personal, EAP, referral) |
| `lead_score` | 0-100 scoring (higher = hotter lead) |
| `status` | `INQUIRY_RECEIVED`, `CONTACTED`, `BOOKED`, `ENROLLED`, `CLOSED` |
| `notes` | Free-text notes |

**Lead Scoring Guide (Oil & Gas):**
- +30: Rotational worker with active substance use concern
- +25: HR/EAP inquiry from O&G company
- +20: Referral from another O&G client
- +15: Visited /programs/oil-gas page
- +10: Downloaded O&G-specific resource
- -10: No response after 3 attempts

**Campaign Tag**: All O&G leads should be tagged `campaign = 'oil-gas'` in the main `lead_pipeline.db`.

## Intake Workflow — O&G Leads

When an oil & gas lead comes in (via website, phone, or email):

1. **Tag campaign** as `oil-gas` in lead_pipeline.db
2. **Score lead** using the guide above
3. **Send Template 2** (direct outreach email) within 1 hour
4. **Log in oil_gas_leads.db** with source, platform, enquiry_type
5. **Book intake call** — offer times that accommodate rotation (early AM, evenings, weekends)
6. **In intake call**, confirm:
   - Current rotation schedule
   - Employer situation (disclosure preference)
   - Substance type and frequency
   - Mental health co-occurring concerns
7. **Assign to 28-Day Virtual Boot Camp** with rotation-adjusted schedule

## Compliance Notes

- Never reference a specific employer in any public-facing content
- Never imply employer-mandated treatment (unless client confirms it)
- Never promise confidentiality from legal/court proceedings
- Always include: *"Treatment is voluntary and confidential. Trifecta does not share information with employers without written client consent."*
- HIPAA/PIPEDA compliant — no PHI in emails, logs, or error messages
- Use person-first language: "person with substance use disorder" not "addict"
