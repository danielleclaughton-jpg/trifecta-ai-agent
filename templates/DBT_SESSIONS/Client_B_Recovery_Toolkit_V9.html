C) Family Boundaries — FAST & GIVE

Use FAST when you must protect values/time/health. Use GIVE when the relationship needs care and buy‑in. Often you’ll use both: GIVE first, then FAST.
FAST — Self‑Respect
F = Fair • A = Apologies (only if yours) • S = Stick to values • T = Truthful
GIVE — Relationship Skill
G = Gentle • I = Interested • V = Validate • E = Easy manner
Decision rule: Relationship long‑term → start with GIVE. Protect time/health/quality → FAST. If both: GIVE 1 line (“I get you’re helping”) + FAST 1 line (“I’m keeping this boundary”).
