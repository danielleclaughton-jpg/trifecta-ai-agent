Section 4: Wise Mind Boundaries for Urges to Reach Out

The Challenge: You'll have strong urges to contact Katie – to explain, seek reassurance, share something, check in, or try to fix things. These urges feel urgent and overwhelming, but they're usually Emotion Mind seeking relief from anxiety or hope.

Scenario 4.1: Urge to Text Katie "Just to Check In"

😰 Emotion Mind Says:

  • "I just want to see how she's doing"
  • "Maybe she's thinking about me too"
  • "A friendly text won't hurt anything"
  • "What if she's waiting for me to reach out?"

🧊 Reasonable Mind Says:

  • "She made her choice. Don't bother"
  • "You're just seeking validation"
  • "Block her and move on"
  • "Checking in is pointless"

🧭 Wise Mind Says:

  • "This urge is anxiety seeking relief, not genuine need"
  • "What am I hoping this text will accomplish?"
  • "Will this serve my long-term well-being or just give temporary relief?"
  • "She has my number. If she wants to connect, she can reach out"
  • "I'll wait 48 hours. If I still feel this urge then, I'll reassess"
URGE TO REACH OUT PROTOCOL:
STEP 1: PAUSE & NAME IT
Say out loud: "I'm having an urge to contact Katie. This is Emotion Mind seeking relief."
STEP 2: ASK WISE MIND QUESTIONS
  • What am I hoping this contact will accomplish?
  • Am I seeking reassurance, connection, or trying to change her mind?
  • Will this serve my long-term recovery and well-being?
  • What does Wise Mind say?
STEP 3: SET A 48-HOUR TIMER
Tell yourself: "If I still genuinely need to contact her in 48 hours, I can revisit this decision."
STEP 4: INTERRUPT THE URGE (Do ONE Now)
  • Call/text a safe person
  • Physical activity (walk, shower, exercise)
  • Read recovery material
  • Journal: "What I'm hoping this text will fix: ___"
  • NA meeting or recovery video
STEP 5: AFTER 48 HOURS, REASSESS
95% of the time, the urge will have passed. If it hasn't, check: Am I in Wise Mind (50%+)? Does this TRULY serve my recovery? Is this about her or about me managing my anxiety?

Scenario 4.2: Urge to Send Something Meaningful (Gift, Letter, Explanation)

😰 Emotion Mind Says:

  • "If I send this heartfelt letter, she'll understand"
  • "A thoughtful gift will show her I care"

Wise Mind Boundary: Do NOT send grand gesture gifts, long letters, explanations, or apologies right now. These are almost always Emotion Mind trying to control the outcome or seeking reassurance. They rarely land the way you hope and often push the other person further away.
ALTERNATIVE TO SENDING:
  • Write it out: Write the full letter, text, or explanation in a journal or document
  • Read it out loud to yourself or a safe person
  • Sit with it for 7 days without sending
  • Ask Wise Mind: "Is this for ME (to process) or for HER (to change her mind)?"
  • If it's for YOU: Keep it. It's done its job.
  • If it's for HER: Don't send it. You're trying to control the outcome.
